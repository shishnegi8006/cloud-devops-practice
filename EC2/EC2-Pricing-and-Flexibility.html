EC2 Pricing & Flexibility

Optimizing Costs and Performance with Instance Models

Purchasing Options

On-Demand Flexible

No upfront commitment. Good for unpredictable workloads. Pay by the second.

Savings Plans / RIs Max Savings

Commit to 1 or 3 years (usage or instance). Up to 72% discount. Steady-state usage.

Spot Instances Cheap

Unused capacity up to 90% off. Can be interrupted with 2-min warning. Stateless apps.

Dedicated Host Compliance

Physical server dedicated to you. BYOL (Bring Your Own License) support.

Placement Groups

Cluster

Single AZ. Low latency, high network throughput. Good for HPC.

Spread

Strict isolation. Each instance on distinct hardware. Max 7 per AZ. Good for critical apps.

Partition

Logical partitions. Instances in one partition don't share hardware with others. Good for Hadoop/Kafka.

Elasticity (ASG)

  • Auto Scaling Groups: Automatically add/remove instances to match load.
  • Scaling Policies:
    • Target Tracking (e.g., maintain 40% CPU)
    • Simple/Step Scaling (add 2 units when alarm breaches)
    • Scheduled Scaling (predictable spikes)
  • Warm Pools: Keep instances in stopped state for faster scale-out.
  • Predictive Scaling: Uses ML to forecast load and schedule capacity.

Exam Strategy Tip

If the scenario mentions "fault-tolerant," "flexible start/end times," or "urgent compute at lowest cost," choose Spot Instances. If it mentions "steady workloads" or "predictable usage," choose Reserved Instances or Savings Plans.
